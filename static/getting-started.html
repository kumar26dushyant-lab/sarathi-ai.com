Create Your Telegram CRM Bot

It's easier than ordering food online — just 3 simple steps!

📲

Step 1: Open Telegram

Open the Telegram app on your phone (or computer). Don't have Telegram? Download it free from Google Play Store or Apple App Store — takes 30 seconds.

💡 Telegram is like WhatsApp but with superpowers — your CRM bot lives here. You'll use it to manage leads, run calculators, and get AI insights.
🤖

Step 2: Search for @BotFather

In Telegram's search bar (top), type @BotFather and open it. BotFather is Telegram's official bot that creates other bots for you. It's free and trusted by millions.

Send this message to BotFather:
/newbot

BotFather will ask two questions:
  • A display name for your bot (e.g., "Apex Financial CRM")
  • A username ending in "bot" (e.g., "ApexInsuranceBot")
🔑

Step 3: Copy the Token & Paste in Sarathi-AI

After creating the bot, BotFather will give you a token — a long code that looks like this:

7123456789:AAF_abc123def456ghi

Copy this token → Go to your Sarathi-AI Onboarding page → Paste it → Click "Connect Bot"
🔒 This token is like a password for your bot. Never share it publicly. Sarathi-AI stores it securely.
👇 This Is Exactly What It Looks Like
🤖 BotFather

ApexInsuranceBot
✅ Done! Your new bot is created.

✅ That's it! Your Telegram CRM Bot is ready!

Option A: WhatsApp Web Link (Simplest — No Setup!)

Sarathi-AI generates "click to send" WhatsApp links. When you tap "Share on WhatsApp" in the bot, it opens WhatsApp with the message ready to send. Zero setup needed!

⭐ This works out of the box — no configuration at all. Perfect for Solo Advisors!
🔗

Option B: WhatsApp Cloud API (Team & Enterprise Plans)

For automatic sending (birthday greetings, renewal reminders without manual tapping), you need the WhatsApp Cloud API. This gives you:

  • Auto birthday wishes — sent at 9 AM, no action needed
  • Bulk greeting messages
  • Branded message templates
How to set up:
1. Go to developers.facebook.com
2. Create a free Developer Account (use your Facebook login)
3. Create a "Business" type app
4. Add "WhatsApp" product
5. Copy the Phone Number ID and Access Token
6. Paste them in your Sarathi-AI settings
💡 Meta (Facebook) gives you 1,000 free messages per month — more than enough for most advisors!
