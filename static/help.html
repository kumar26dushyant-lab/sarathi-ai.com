How do I sign up for Sarathi-AI? ▼
  1. Visit our homepage and scroll to the pricing section
  2. Click "Start Free Trial" on your preferred plan
  3. Fill in your firm name, your name, phone number, and email
  4. Click "Start My Free Trial" — you'll get 14 days free, no credit card needed
  5. You'll be redirected to the onboarding page to complete setup
What happens during onboarding? ▼

Onboarding has 2 simple steps:

  1. Telegram Bot: Create a bot via @BotFather on Telegram, then paste the token in Sarathi-AI. This becomes your personal CRM bot.
  2. Branding: Add your tagline, contact details, and credentials. These appear on calculators and reports you share with clients.
How do I create a Telegram bot? ▼
  1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather
  2. Send /newbot
  3. Give your bot a name (e.g., "MyFirm Insurance Bot")
  4. Give it a username ending in "bot" (e.g., myfirm_insurance_bot)
  5. BotFather will give you a token like 1234567890:ABCdef...
  6. Copy this token and paste it in the Sarathi-AI onboarding page
💡 Keep your bot token secret! Don't share it with anyone.

🎙️ Voice-First CRM (AI-Powered)

What is Voice-First CRM? ▼

Voice-First CRM lets you manage your entire business by just speaking into your Telegram bot. No typing needed! Just record a voice note and Sarathi-AI will understand what you want to do.

6 Voice Actions Available:

  • Create Lead: "Naya client Priya, phone 9876543210, health insurance chahiye"
  • Log Meeting: "Ramesh ji se mila, health insurance discuss kiya, next week follow up karna hai"
  • Update Stage: "Priya ka stage contacted pe update karo"
  • Create Reminder: "Kal shaam ko Suresh ji ko call karna hai"
  • Add Note: "Ramesh ji ne bola ki wife ke liye bhi policy chahiye"
  • General: Any other voice note — AI shows you options to create lead, log meeting, or set reminder
💡 Speak naturally in Hindi, English, or Hinglish — the AI understands all three! Perfect for field advisors on the go.
How do I use voice to create a lead? ▼
  1. Open your Telegram CRM bot
  2. Record a voice note saying something like: "New client Mohan Kumar, phone 98765 43210, Delhi, health insurance chahiye, budget 500 per month"
  3. AI extracts all details automatically — name, phone, city, need, budget, follow-up date
  4. You see a confirmation card — tap ✅ Create Lead or ✏️ Fill Missing to add any details the AI couldn't catch
💡 You can also say dates and times naturally: "kal follow up 4 baje", "agle hafte subah 10 baje", "next Monday at 3 PM" — AI understands dates AND times!
How does voice meeting logging work? ▼

After a client meeting, just record a voice note:

"Aaj Sharma ji se mila unke office mein, term plan discuss kiya 1 crore ka, interested hain, agle Tuesday ko follow up karna hai"

Sarathi-AI will automatically:

  • Find "Sharma ji" in your lead database (fuzzy matching)
  • Log the meeting with a summary
  • Set a follow-up reminder for Tuesday
What happens if voice doesn't work well on slow network? ▼

Sarathi-AI is built for Indian networks! It automatically:

  • Shows "Processing... might take a few seconds" so you know it's working
  • Retries automatically if network drops — up to 2 retries
  • Keeps you updated: "Network hiccup! Retrying..."
💡 Voice notes work even on 2G-level connectivity. Just speak clearly and wait for the response.

📋 Using the CRM (via Telegram)

How do I add a new lead? ▼

You have multiple ways to add leads:

  • Voice: Record a voice note with client details (fastest!)
  • Command: Type /addlead — the bot asks step-by-step for details
  • Dashboard: Use the web dashboard to add leads via the "+ Add Lead" button
  • CSV Import: Upload a CSV file to import multiple leads at once (owners only)
How do I log a policy? ▼

When a lead converts to a customer, use:

/addpolicy

Enter the policy details: type (life/health/motor/etc.), premium amount, start date, and the lead's name. The bot will track renewals automatically.

How do follow-up reminders work? ▼

Sarathi-AI has a complete follow-up accountability system:

  • ⏰ 30-min Pre-Reminders: Set a follow-up with time (e.g. 'kal 4 baje Sharma ji ko call') and get a reminder 30 minutes before — with ✅ Done / 🕐 Snooze buttons
  • 📋 Post Follow-up Notes: After tapping Done, Sarathi-AI asks 'How did it go?' — send a voice note or text to record notes on the lead's timeline
  • 📊 Lead Timeline: Every interaction, note, and follow-up is tracked on a visual timeline — visible on your dashboard
  • 🔄 Smart Nudges: Morning agenda, afternoon reminders, and evening missed follow-up alerts — delivered to your Telegram
  • 🎂 Birthdays & Renewals: Auto birthday greetings and policy renewal reminders (30-day advance)
  • 🎙️ Voice Reminders: Say "kal Suresh ko call karna hai 4 baje" — AI creates a timed reminder automatically!
💡 All reminders come to your Telegram bot with action buttons — no extra app needed! Team plan owners also get a daily accountability digest.
How do I view my dashboard? ▼

Your dashboard is available at the website. It shows:

  • Total leads, policies, and premium collected

  • Pipeline breakdown by stage
  • Lead Timeline: Click 📋 on any lead to see full activity history — interactions, notes, follow-up status
  • AI tools, calculators, and team management (for owners)

🔐 Security & Privacy

Is my data secure? ▼

Absolutely. Sarathi-AI uses multiple layers of security:

  • JWT Authentication: Secure token-based login with OTP verification
  • Tenant Isolation: Each firm's data is completely isolated — no cross-access
  • Encrypted Communication: All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS
  • Abuse Detection: Automated content filtering and progressive block system
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute-force and abuse
How is my client data protected? ▼

Client data (leads, policies) is stored in an encrypted database and is only accessible by your firm's authenticated users. We never share, sell, or access your client data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
