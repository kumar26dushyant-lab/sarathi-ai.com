📋 Common Questions

How quickly will I get a response? ▼

We aim to respond to all tickets within 24 hours on business days. Priority and urgent tickets are handled first.

Can I submit a ticket without an account? ▼

Yes! Just fill in your contact details (name, phone, or email) when submitting the form. We'll reach out to you directly.

How do I check the status of my ticket? ▼

If you're logged in, go to the "My Tickets" tab to see all your tickets and their current status. You can also reply to tickets directly.

What if my bot stops working? ▼

Submit a ticket with category "Technical Issue" and priority "High". Include your firm name and the error you're seeing. Our team will investigate and restart your bot if needed.

How do I upgrade or change my plan? ▼

Submit a ticket with category "Billing & Subscription" and mention the plan you want to switch to. We'll process the change and adjust your billing accordingly.

I forgot my bot token — what do I do? ▼

Go to @BotFather on Telegram, use /mybots, select your bot, and click "API Token" to see it again. Then update it in your dashboard. If you still have trouble, submit a support ticket and we'll help.

How do I contact support directly? ▼

You can reach us at support@sarathi-ai.com or message @SarathiBizBot on Telegram. For fastest response, submit a ticket here.
