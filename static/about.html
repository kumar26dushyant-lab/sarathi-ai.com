Why Sarathi-AI exists

India's advisors deserve an assistant they can talk to.

Almost every advisor we met runs their entire business on three tools: a thick paper register, dozens of WhatsApp chats, and an Excel sheet that crashes every Diwali. Renewals get forgotten. Lakhs in commission slip away. Promising leads go cold under 400 unread messages.

"India's advisors don't need another dashboard. They need an assistant they can talk to — in Hindi, in Hinglish, while driving between meetings — that just gets things done."

That's the whole reason Sarathi-AI exists: to give every advisor — from a one-person agency in a Tier-3 town to a 50-agent firm in a metro — the same voice-first AI that enterprise CRMs charge ₹50,000+ per seat for, at ₹199/month.
