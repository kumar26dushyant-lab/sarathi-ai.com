WhatsApp Sharing & Messages

Send professional messages to clients — from Telegram

This is where Telegram and WhatsApp work together beautifully. You type commands in Telegram, your client receives polished messages on WhatsApp.

💬 /wa 5 Hello Rajesh! WhatsApp
Send a direct WhatsApp message to lead #5. Just type the lead ID and your message. The bot sends it via WhatsApp API and logs it as an interaction.
📊 /wacalc 5 WhatsApp
Send a calculator report directly to lead #5's WhatsApp. Pick a calculator type, and a personalized "Dear Rajesh…" report is sent with your firm branding.
📋 /wadash 5 WhatsApp
Send the client's full policy portfolio summary to their WhatsApp — all policies with insurer, plan, cover, premium, and renewal dates.
🎉 /greet 5 WhatsApp
Send a branded greeting: 🎂 Birthday, 💍 Anniversary, 🙏 Thank You, or 🎉 Festival wishes — with your firm branding, directly on WhatsApp.

How the Telegram → WhatsApp Flow Works

1

You type a command in Telegram

Example: /wacalc 5 to send a calculator report to lead Rajesh.

2

Bot runs the calculator and prepares the message

The report is personalized: "Dear Rajesh, here's your Inflation Impact analysis from [Your Firm Name]…"

3

Message sent via WhatsApp API

Rajesh receives a professional, branded message on WhatsApp — instantly.

4

Interaction logged automatically

The CRM records this as a "WhatsApp" interaction on Rajesh's lead card. No manual logging needed.

⚡ Zero-Setup Option: Even without WhatsApp API, the "Share WhatsApp" button on calculators generates a pre-filled wa.me link that opens WhatsApp with the message ready to send. Perfect for Solo plan users.

Voice-to-Action

Record a voice note → AI creates a lead with follow-up

Just finished a meeting? Record a quick voice note in Hindi, English, or Hinglish. Gemini AI transcribes it, extracts details, and creates a lead — hands-free.

1

Hold the mic button in Telegram

Record up to 2 minutes: "Met Rajesh Kumar today at his shop in Andheri, phone 98765-43210, interested in health insurance for family of 4, budget around 15000, follow up next Tuesday"

2

AI transcribes and extracts entities

Name: Rajesh Kumar, Phone: 9876543210, City: Andheri, Need: Health Insurance, Budget: ₹15,000, Follow-up: Next Tuesday.

3

Confirmation card appears

Review the extracted data and tap ✅ Create Lead or ❌ Discard.

4

Lead created + follow-up scheduled

The lead is added to your pipeline and a reminder is set for Tuesday. Zero typing required.

🗣️ Multilingual: Speak in Hindi, English, or mixed Hinglish — the AI understands all three. Relative dates like "kal" (tomorrow), "agle hafte" (next week), or "Friday" are automatically converted to exact dates.

Frequently Asked Questions

🤔 Do my clients need to install Telegram?

▼
No! Your clients never see Telegram. They receive all messages on WhatsApp, which they already use daily. Telegram is only your private command center — like a cockpit that only the pilot sees.

💰 Is Telegram free to use?

▼
Yes, completely free. Telegram has no per-message charges, no 24-hour window rules, and no template approvals. You can send unlimited commands to your bot at zero cost. (WhatsApp API incurs Meta's per-conversation charges, but the Telegram side is always free.)

📱 Can I use Telegram on my computer?

▼
Yes! Telegram works on phone, tablet, Windows/Mac desktop app, and web browser — all simultaneously. Your CRM chat syncs across every device. Many advisors prefer the desktop app for faster typing.

🔒 Is my data safe on Telegram?

▼
Your data is stored on our secure servers, not on Telegram. The bot is just the interface — like a remote control for your CRM. Messages between you and the bot are encrypted. Each firm's data is completely isolated (multi-tenant).

🌐 Does it work in Hindi?

▼
Yes! Type /lang to switch to Hindi (हिन्दी). All bot messages, menu buttons, and calculator results switch to Hindi. Voice-to-action also understands Hindi and Hinglish.

⚡ What if I'm in the middle of something and get interrupted?

▼
All wizards (add lead, follow-up, claim, etc.) have a 30-minute timeout. You can resume anytime within that window. Type /cancel to abort any wizard and start fresh.

📊 Can I see everything on a web dashboard too?

▼
Absolutely. Type /dashboard in Telegram and it shows a summary + link to the full web dashboard. The web dashboard gives you charts, tables, and exportable reports. Telegram and web always show the same data.

🏢 Can each agent in my firm use the bot independently?

▼
Yes. Each agent joins via an invite code and gets their own isolated workspace within your firm. They see only their own leads and pipeline. Owners/admins can view all agents' data and transfer data if an agent leaves.
