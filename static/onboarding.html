🤖 Step 1: Create Your Telegram CRM Bot

Your firm gets its own branded Telegram bot — your agents use it daily to manage leads, pipeline, and follow-ups.

📋 How to create your bot (2 minutes)

1. Open Telegram and search for @BotFather

2. Send /newbot to BotFather

3. Choose a display name for your bot (e.g., Acme Financial CRM)

4. Choose a username ending in "bot" (e.g., AcmeInsuranceBot)

5. BotFather will give you an API Token — copy it and paste below

Open @BotFather on Telegram

• Use your firm name as the bot name — your clients will see it in WhatsApp-style notifications

• Example: "Krishna Insurance Advisor" or "Sharma Financial Services Bot"
